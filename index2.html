Our Vision

To be the leading Eco-Friendly Cleaning and Landscaping services provider.


Our Mission

is to positively impact the quality of life of:

The business partners we service by delivering detail oriented, consistent service
People we employ by ensuring safety at work and giving them reasonable wages
Future generations by using environmentally friendly products.


Our Passion for the Environment

We are committed to using products that are environmentally responsible,
sustainable, and safe for the Earth.


Safety and Sustainability

We are committed to safeguarding the health and wellbeing of our clients,
our staff and the communities where we carry our operations.


Our Core Values

Innovation- Exhibiting curiosity and committing to exploration

Consistent Quality – Delivering quality results consistently
Safety and sustainability – Safeguarding the health and wellbeing of our clients, our staff and the environment
Commitment – Calling on passion and persistence to deliver excellence in operations
Integrity – Demonstrating Openness and honesty at all times
Knowledge – Fostering a culture that encourages learning and personal development. Being experts in our field
Ownership – Empowering staff to be accountable and responsible
